Rules

Turns

  • Tile-Jump is played by two players taking turns.
  • During each turn, a player selects one of their jumpers and moves it one, two or three times.
  • Whenever a jumper leaves a tile, that tile is permanently destroyed.
  • Jumpers can't pass through destroyed tiles or other jumpers (belonging to any player).
  • If any of a player's jumpers has no valid moves at the start of their turn, that player loses.
  • If any of a player's jumpers starts their turn on a lava tile, that player loses.

Movement

  • Once a jumper is selected during a players turn, that jumper moves one, two or three times.
  • During a move, a jumper travels along a straight line (horizontally or diagonally) until blocked by a wall or another jumper.
